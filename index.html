About

Light as a
first language.

I'm a Portland-based photographer drawn to the in-between — the pause before a laugh, the last light on an empty street, the way stillness can hold an entire mood.

My work spans portraiture, documentary, and editorial. Whether shooting on film or digital, I approach every frame with patience. I'm not chasing the decisive moment — I'm waiting for the honest one.
